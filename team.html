Samuel NIYOMUREMYI

Samuel NIYOMUREMYI is Founder/Chief Executive of Ubuzima Innovation Hub, Chairperson of Rwanda Catalyst 2030, NGO endorsed under global movement of Catalyst 2030 to achieve UN SDGS. He has scaled various projects and his Global leadership was recognized by One Young World Manchester delegate 2022 and Unleash Innovation Lab India 2022 as Global Talent. And now He is fellow of Clinton Global Initiative University 2023

Sauteur NDIKUBWIMANA

Sauteur, Deputy Executive Director at Ubuzima Innovation Hub, earned his Bachelors’ degrees in Clinical Psychology from the University of Rwanda. His interests are in Youth and adolescent mental health and community engagement activities. Mr. Sauteur has earned a various certificate, while he was a president of Clinical Psychology Students’ Association of Rwanda (CPSAR) with a particular focus on Mental Health Promotion. In 2021-2022, He was a president of CPSAR and organizer of National Youth conference 2022 which was organized by CPSAR, Embassy of Israel in Rwanda, UNICEF Rwanda and University of Rwanda. Mr. Sauteur is licensed Junior Clinical Psychologist.He is a Bilingual in English and Kinyarwanda.

INEZA H Vainqueur
